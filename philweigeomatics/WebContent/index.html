My Hobbies

Ever since I was a child, my dad has been pointing at cars on the street and telling me what they are. He is such a faithful car lover, so he made me just like him; but there remains one big difference between him and I. I simply just love everything that has wheels.

When I was 13, I started to fall in love with Bike Trials. At the time, I was one of the few people in my hometown adopting this fantasitc sport and I was the youngest. Two years later, I became the team leader in our bicycle club and managed most of the team activities including performance shows, advertisement and so on. After moving to Beijing for high school in 2005, there had been very little time to keep myself on the saddle.

Moving to Canada seems to help a lot with my hobbies. I have self-modified a 2004 RX8 which I sold in 2009 and now the picture shown is my new toy bought in 2010.

My Works Explained

GIS stands Geographic Information System, that is used to deal with geospatial data in a variety of methods and in many different kinds of representations. GIS is being used in many different applications and industries. There are also many GISs available nowadays including the industry-leading Arc Suite developed by ESRI; they provide a set of relatively complete solutions for all types of Geospatial Information related works but since they target enterprise solutions, they are hardly affordable by smaller teams. There are also open souce or free GISs available such as Quantum GIS (Open Source), Grass GIS (Open Source), Open Jump (Open Source) and so on. For a more complete list of solutions, please visit List of geographic information systems software on Wikipedia.

More and more applications being developed with Spatial Information built-in. With the evolution of mobile technologies, a terminology named LBS (Location Based Services) has been widely adopted by mobile applications.

"Open Street Map (OSM) is a collaborative project to create a free editable map of the world." This is the definition to Open Street Map provided by wikipedia. The map can be edited and the data can be collected by everyone. It highly utilized the public power and it leads a benefit that the map data is completely free to use. The opposite side is that the data accuracy is not guaranteed. I have been an active OSM participant since 2011 to contribute to the project and the data I have contributed to has been mainly in Alberta area.
